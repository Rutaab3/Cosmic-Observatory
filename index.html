Cosmic Timeline

Journey through the history of the universe

Big Bang

13.8 billion years ago

The universe begins with a singularity that rapidly expands, creating space, time, and matter.

First Stars

13.6 billion years ago

The first generation of stars forms from primordial hydrogen and helium gas.

First Galaxies

13.2 billion years ago

Gravity pulls matter together to form the first galaxies and galaxy clusters.

Solar System Formation

4.6 billion years ago

Our solar system forms from a collapsing cloud of gas and dust.

Life on Earth

3.8 billion years ago

The first signs of life appear on Earth in the form of simple microorganisms.
